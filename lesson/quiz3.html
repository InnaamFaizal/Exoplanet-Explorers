Lesson Quiz: Exoplanets

Question 1: What is the primary condition necessary for life on an exoplanet?

A) Solid surface
B) Liquid water
C) High temperatures
D) Low pressure

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: B)

Question 2: What is the term for signs of life detectable in a planet's atmosphere or surface?

A) Biosignatures
B) Habitability indicators
C) Life markers
D) Planetary signals

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: A)

Question 3: Which factor influences the habitable zone around a star?

A) Star size and age
B) Planet size and composition
C) Distance from the star
D) All of the above

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: D)

Question 4: What type of organisms can survive in temperatures ranging from -20°C to 120°C and pressures up to 1000 times that of Earth's surface?

A) Thermophilic bacteria
B) Tardigrades
C) Extremophiles
D) Both A and B

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: C)

Question 5: Which exoplanet is located in the habitable zone of a G-type star and has a radius 1.6 times that of Earth?

A) Kepler-452b
B) Proxima Centauri b
C) TRAPPIST-1e
D) KELT-9b

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: A)

Lesson Quiz: Exoplanets

Question 1: What is another term for exoplanets?

A) Extrasolar planets
B) Interstellar planets
C) Cosmic planets
D) Galactic planets

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: A)

Question 2: How many exoplanets have been confirmed as of now?

A) Over 1,000
B) Over 5,600
C) Over 10,000
D) Over 50,000

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: B)

Question 3: What is the primary purpose of the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) in exoplanet research?

A) To detect exoplanets using the transit method
B) To study the atmospheres of exoplanets and search for signs of life
C) To directly image Earth-like planets
D) To measure the radial velocity of stars

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: B)

Question 4: What is the main challenge in using the Direct Imaging method to detect exoplanets?

A) The star's light overpowers the planet's signal
B) The planet's orbit is too distant from the star
C) The planet's atmosphere is too dense
D) The star's radial velocity is too small

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: A)

Question 5: How does the Radial Velocity Method detect exoplanets?

A) By measuring the dimming of a star's light
B) By capturing images of exoplanets
C) By measuring the wobble in a star's position caused by an orbiting planet
D) By analyzing the spectrum of light from a star

Right Answer!

Correct Answer: C)
